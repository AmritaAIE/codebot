ABOUT CODEBOT

CodeBOT Hackathon is a unique two-day event that combines the power of coding,
prompt engineering, and large language models to tackle
the challenges posed by today's environmental issues.

With a focus on the theme "One Earth, One Family, One Future," which aligns with this year's
G20 agenda, this hackathon empowers participants to leverage cutting-edge technology
for a sustainable and inclusive future.

This is a one of its kind, pioneering college event that explores the potential of large language models for environmental applications. Be part of a community of passionate individuals determined to make a positive change, and together, let's create a better world for generations to come.
